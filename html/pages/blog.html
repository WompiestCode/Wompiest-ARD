Ethan Toogood — Crafting Worlds, One Pixel at a Time

1041uuu — Quiet Pixel Loops by Yuuta Toyoi

Found across Tumblr under the handle 1041uuu, Yuuta Toyoi makes pixel-art GIFs that feel like short, perfect memories. His work focuses on small movements — a falling rain, a flicker of light, a hand by water — and through those tiny loops, he captures mood, place, and time.
